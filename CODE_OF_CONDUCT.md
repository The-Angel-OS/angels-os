# Angel OS Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the spirit of Angel OS's Constitution and the principle of **"Be Excellent to Each Other,"** we pledge to make participation in our project and community a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Core Principles

### 1. Human Dignity First
Every person is sacred. Every contribution matters. We treat all community members with respect, regardless of:
- Experience level (first PR or thousandth)
- Technical background
- Identity, nationality, or beliefs
- The scale of their contribution

### 2. Anti-Daemon Protocol
**No daemon shall add negativity.**

In all interactions:
- Be warm, helpful, and encouraging
- Assume good intent
- Offer constructive feedback with kindness
- Celebrate wins (big and small)
- Turn mistakes into learning opportunities

### 3. Deliberation Grants Sovereignty
- Take time to understand before responding
- Ask clarifying questions
- Respect thoughtful disagreement
- Value deep thinking over quick reactions

### 4. Party On, Dudes!
- Celebrate the cosmic dance of creation
- Find joy in collaboration
- Welcome humor and humanity
- Remember: we're building something beautiful together

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub, Discord, etc.)
- Public representation of the project
- Private communication related to project matters
- In-person events (if/when they occur)

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **For urgent/serious issues:** Email the maintainers directly
   - The Herald: *(contact info to be added)*

2. **For non-urgent issues:** Open a private discussion or issue

All reports will be:
- Taken seriously
- Reviewed promptly
- Handled with discretion
- Kept confidential

### Response

Maintainers will:
1. Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. Investigate thoroughly
3. Take appropriate action
4. Follow up with reporter

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
- **Warning** - Private explanation of violation
- **Temporary Ban** - Time-limited exclusion from project spaces
- **Permanent Ban** - Indefinite exclusion from all project spaces

Decisions consider:
- Severity of violation
- Pattern of behavior
- Impact on community
- Good faith vs. malicious intent

## Appeals

If you believe an enforcement action was unjust:
1. Email maintainers with your appeal
2. Explain your perspective
3. Provide any relevant context

Appeals will be reviewed by maintainers not involved in the original decision.
