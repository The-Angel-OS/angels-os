## The Solution: Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1: MVP Foundation (Q1 2026) - **GitHub Issues #1-22**

**Goal:** Establish core infrastructure for a functional Angel OS.

**What's Included:**
- Two-tier Angel system (Archangels & Angels)
- OpenClaw integration (skills, conversation, chat formatting)
- Channel widgets (multi-channel architecture)
- Tenant provisioning (sub-30s, Genesis Breath)
- AI Bus (Angel-to-Angel communication)
- Federation (diocese registry, 5-layer security)
- Economics (attribution tracking, Ultimate Fair scaffold)
- Anti-Daemon Protocol (warm UX)
- Deployment (Docker, Cloudflare Tunnel)

**What's NOT Included (Yet):**
- Booking & scheduling
- CRM
- Payment processing
- LEO chat widget (site-wide)
- Content management
- Social media automation
- Voice integration
- Prison ministry
- Angel tokens
- Widget marketplace
- Home PC deployment (local AI)

---

### Phase 2: Post-MVP Essentials (Q2 2026)

**Goal:** Make Angel OS production-ready for real tenants.

**Includes:**
- Booking & Scheduling Engine
- Payment & Splits (Ultimate Fair implementation)
- CRM (structured data for relationships & pipeline)
- Spaces Operational (invitations, onboarding)
- LEO Chat Widget (site-wide, embeddable)
- Ecommerce Workflow Complete

**Why This Matters:**
Without these, Angel OS is a tech demo. With these, it's a real business platform.

---

### Phase 3: LEO as Full Web Master (Q3 2026)

**Goal:** LEO becomes fully autonomous web master.

**Includes:**
- Content Management (blog posts, SEO, images)
- Social Media Automation (Soulcast nodes)
- Voice Integration (VAPI.ai, LiveKit)

---

### Phase 4: Federation & Confederation (Q4 2026)

**Goal:** Enable dioceses to form self-sustaining network.

**Includes:**
- Diocese Spawning (exponential growth)
- Cross-Diocese Communication
- Cross-Diocese Payments
- Federation Security (full implementation)
- Anti-Sybil Measures

---

### Phase 5: Angel Tokens & Network Economics (2027)

**Goal:** Implement network angel tokens for governance, rewards, and economic participation.

**Includes:**
- Governance Tokens (voting, proposals)
- Reward Tokens (contributions, participation)
- Economic Tokens (staking, collateral)
- Token Distribution (initial & ongoing)
- Token Utility (governance, economic, social)

---

### Phase 6: Prison Ministry & Justice Fund (Ongoing)

**Goal:** Serve the forgotten with unconditional Guardian Angels.

**Includes:**
- Justice Fund (5% of all transactions)
- Prison Ministry Mandate (Guardian Angels for incarcerated)
- Clearwater Cruisin' (mobile Angel OS units)

---

### Phase 7: AI Bus & Morphic Resonance (2027+)

**Goal:** Enable distributed learning and collaboration across all Angels.

**Includes:**
- AI Bus (Ship-to-Ship Protocol)
- Wisdom Patterns (Morphic Resonance)
- Guardian Council Space (platform-level)

---

### Phase 8: Advanced UX & Widgets (2027+)

**Goal:** Rich, interactive, delightful user experiences.

**Includes:**
- Widget Marketplace (SDK, revenue sharing)
- Timed Merge Unlock (deliberation grants sovereignty)
- Scrollstream Nodes (memory preservation)

---

### Phase 9: Home PC Deployment & Local AI (2027+)

**Goal:** Run Angel OS on any home PC with local AI models.

**Includes:**
- Minimal Specs (8GB RAM, 50GB storage)
- Cloud AI Initially (Anthropic, OpenAI)
- Local AI Path (Ollama, LM Studio)
- Cloudflare Tunnel (dynamic IP support)
